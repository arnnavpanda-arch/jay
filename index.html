Delivery Staff Eligibility

Basic Qualification

  • Minimum 10th Pass (Matriculation).
  • 12th Pass or higher is preferred but not mandatory.

Age & Physical Requirements

  • 18 to 45 years. Physically fit for delivery work.
  • Able to lift and carry delivery packages safely.
  • Willing to work in different weather conditions.

Driving Requirements

  • Valid Two-Wheeler Driving License (Mandatory).
  • Good riding skills and knowledge of traffic rules.
  • Own bike/scooty preferred (or agency-provided vehicle if applicable).

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Driving License, Passport-size Photograph, Bank Account Details, Mobile Number, Address Proof

Skills

  • Basic smartphone knowledge, able to use GPS/Google Maps, good communication, honest, punctual.

Technical Staff Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • Computer Course / Diploma in Computer Science, IT, Electronics, or a related field.
  • B.Tech, BCA, B.Sc (Computer Science), MCA.
  • Final-year students with relevant skills may also apply as interns or trainees.

Age

  • 18 to 55 years.

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Educational Certificates, Passport-size Photograph, Resume/CV, Bank Account Details, Mobile Number, Address Proof

Technical Skills

  • Computer Hardware & Troubleshooting, Networking (Wi-Fi), Windows Use, Mobile app support, Printer/barcode scanner/POS setup.

Soft Skills & Experience

  • Good communication, problem-solving, teamwork, time management, customer support.
  • Freshers with strong technical knowledge are welcome.

Webcam Staff Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • Minimum 12th Pass. Graduate (Any Stream) preferred.
  • Candidates with BCA, B.Tech, BBA, or equivalent qualifications are an advantage.

Age

  • 18 to 55 years.

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Educational Certificates, Passport-size Photograph, Resume/CV, Bank Account Details, Mobile Number, Address Proof

Technical Requirements

  • Basic computer proficiency, Knowledge of Microsoft Office.
  • Ability to operate webcam and Barcode QR code scan.
  • Fast typing and accurate data entry, Stable internet connection.

Skills & Experience

  • Good English and local language communication, professional behavior, attention to detail.
  • Freshers are welcome.

Finance Manager Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • Minimum Bachelor’s Degree in Commerce, Finance, Accounting, Business Administration.
  • Preferred: B.Com, M.Com, BBA (Finance), MBA (Finance), CA/CMA (Inter) or equivalent.

Age

  • 21 to 55 years.

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Educational/Experience Certificates, Passport-size Photograph, Resume, Bank Account, Mobile, Address Proof

Technical Skills

  • Accounting, financial reporting, budget planning, payroll, Advanced Excel, Tally ERP, GST.

Soft Skills & Experience

  • Strong analytical skills, leadership, decision-making, high level of integrity.
  • Minimum 2–5 years in finance, accounting, or financial management.

Auditor Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • Minimum Bachelor’s Degree in Commerce, Accounting, Finance, Business Administration.
  • Preferred: B.Com, M.Com, BBA, MBA (Finance), CA/CMA (Inter/Final).

Age

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Educational/Experience Certificates, Passport-size Photograph, Resume, Bank Account, Mobile, Address Proof

Soft Skills & Experience

Soft Skills & Experience
